Welcome at GDAC

What is the progress of your business?

GDAC is a tax and accounting firm that has made its mark by its distinctive approach. We focus on personalized advice in the economic, social, legal and tax fields. For us, it is indispensable to know our client's activity in order to advise him in the right way.

Our philosophy is to understand the framework in which the client operates at its best. Guide him in his daily tasks. Optimize the workflow and administrative processes while respecting the budget. Advise him proactively. Furthermore, we have a continuous recruitment process to accommodate our growth.

Our challenge is to form a polyvalent team and become a relevant office in our sector.

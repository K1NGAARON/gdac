Jérémie Godu

Managing Partner – Tax advisor & Accountant ITAA

I started in 2000 at then big 5 Arthur Andersen, specifically in the BPO (business process outsourcing) department, as a young accounting-tax graduate. Afterwards, I worked for a while at Deloitte as an accountant and, while working, obtained my degree in commercial sciences, in addition to that of accountant-tax specialist. After an assignment as financial controller, I decided to become a 100% accountant. I obtained my expertise in 2006 and have been working as an accountant ever since. I have therefore worked in Finance for my entire career. I am married, have a lovely wife and 6 cool kids and am plus dad of my wife's 5 kids. Besides working and taking care of my family, I am a drummer in a cover band and enjoy sports. ‘Play hard, work hard’ is therefore a fitting motto. I am also the creator of the motto ‘Thinking together’, as dialogue between different actors usually leads to the solution.
